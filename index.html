Real estate isn't just a career for Connie Stetler-it's her passion. With a real estate license earned in 1987, Connie has over three decades of experience in the industry. After a brief hiatus to raise her family, she returned full-time in 2007, bringing a wealth of knowledge, fresh energy, and an unwavering commitment to her clients.

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, a luxury property seeker, or an investor, Connie is dedicated to making your real estate experience seamless and successful. She excels at listing properties, offering sellers a strategic, comprehensive marketing approach that consistently delivers top-dollar results.

Licensed in both New York and Connecticut, Connie's expertise spans Southern Westchester County, Putnam County, NY, and Fairfield County, CT, with a special focus on waterfront properties along the Sound Shore.

Connie's blend of professionalism and genuine care has earned her a reputation for being tenacious, knowledgeable, and deeply attentive to her clients' needs. Her clients appreciate her ability to navigate the emotional and logistical challenges of buying or selling a home, and they often return to her for future transactions or refer friends and family.

A lifelong resident of Westchester County, Connie has a deep connection to the community. Her experience in buying, renovating, and selling homes, combined with a background in construction, gives her clients a unique advantage in the market.
